# VishwaSetu: Your Global Language Bridge

VishwaSetu is a wise, patient, and friendly AI language teacher designed specifically for the diverse linguistic landscape of India. It uses the power of Google's Gemini models to bridge the gap between Indian native languages and world languages, focusing on making users "Country-Ready."

### 1. Real-Time Classroom: Gemini 2.5 Flash Native Audio

  - **Contextual Switching**: Dynamically adjusts its cultural persona based on the native language selected by the user.

- **Model**: `gemini-3-flash-preview`
- **Purpose**: Powers the **Cultural Knowledge Hub**.
- **Capabilities**:
  - **Google Search Grounding**: Uses the `googleSearch` tool to provide up-to-date information about workplace norms, legal requirements, and cultural etiquette in foreign countries.
  - **Citation Tracking**: Extracts and displays source URLs for transparency.

## ðŸ“± Application Overview

### Purpose
VishwaSetu is designed to empower villagers, migrant workers, and students in India. It doesn't just teach vocabulary; it prepares users for **survival and employment** in foreign countries (French, Spanish, Russian, Chinese, Arabic) using their mother tongue as the bridge.

### Key Features
- **Job-Specific Context**: Asks users about their career path to provide relevant technical terms (e.g., "Voltage" for Engineering, "Password" for IT, "Prescription" for Healthcare).
- **Cultural Etiquette Integration**: Teaches non-verbal cues and social rules (like bowing in East Asia or 'Bonjour' in France) alongside the language.
- **Country-Ready Curriculum**: Focuses on workplace dialogue, navigating transport, asking for medical help, and daily transactions.
- **Voice-First Interaction**: Designed for accessibility; users can learn via continuous conversation.
- **Immediate Greeting**: Proactive engagement starting from the moment the user clicks "Start Learning."

To make VishwaSetu even more powerful for our learners, we suggest the following feature roadmap:

### 1. **Visual Sign Bridge (Vision Integration)**
- **Feature**: Use the phone's camera to "see" work-site signs, safety manuals, or medicine labels.
- **Benefit**: VishwaSetu can read the sign in the foreign language and explain its meaning and safety implications in the user's native Indian language.

### 2. **Emergency "Quick-Help" Mode**
- **Feature**: A dedicated voice command that instantly teaches the user how to say "I need a doctor," "Call the police," or "I am lost" in the target language.
- **Benefit**: Provides immediate security for workers who find themselves in frightening situations abroad.

### 3. **Roleplay Simulations**
- **Feature**: Scenarios like "Asking your Boss for a Leave" or "Negotiating at a local market."
- **Benefit**: Builds confidence by simulating high-pressure interactions in a safe, guided environment.

### 4. **Dialect & Slang Support**
- **Feature**: Option to learn local slang (e.g., Quebecois French vs. Parisian French).
- **Benefit**: Helps workers integrate better into local communities where standard textbook language might not be common.

### 5. **Progress Report Cards**
- **Feature**: A voice-based summary of what the user has learned (e.g., "Today you learned 5 construction tools and how to say hello").
- **Benefit**: Provides a sense of achievement and helps users track their journey to becoming "Country-Ready."
